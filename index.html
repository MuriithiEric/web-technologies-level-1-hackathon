WEB HACKATHON LEVEL 1

A web hackathon is an event where individuals or teams come together to collaboratively work on projects related to web development within a limited timeframe, typically ranging from a few hours to a few days. Participants, often including programmers, designers, and project managers, gather to brainstorm ideas, build prototypes, and ultimately create functional web applications or websites. These events foster creativity, innovation, and teamwork, as participants are encouraged to push the boundaries of their technical skills while tackling real-world challenges or exploring new technologies. Hackathons often feature workshops, mentorship sessions, and networking opportunities, providing participants with a supportive environment to learn, grow, and showcase their talents. At the end of the hackathon, projects are usually presented to a panel of judges or the broader community, with prizes awarded to standout creations. Overall, web hackathons serve as exciting platforms for skill development, community engagement, and the rapid iteration of web-based solutions.
